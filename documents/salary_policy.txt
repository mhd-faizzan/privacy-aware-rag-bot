SALARY POLICY - CONFIDENTIAL - MANAGER ACCESS ONLY

Salary Bands 2025:
- Junior Engineer: $60,000 - $80,000
- Mid Engineer: $80,000 - $110,000
- Senior Engineer: $110,000 - $150,000
- Manager: $130,000 - $180,000

Bonus Structure:
- Performance bonus: up to 15% of base salary
- Managers are eligible for additional 10% leadership bonus
- Bonuses are reviewed every Q4

Salary Review Process:
- Annual reviews happen every January
- Mid-year adjustments allowed for promotions only
- All salary changes must be approved by HR and Finance

This document is restricted to managers and HR personnel only.
