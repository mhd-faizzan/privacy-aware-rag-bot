EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - ALL STAFF

Welcome to the team! This handbook covers everything you need to know as an employee.

Working Hours:
- Core hours are 10am to 4pm in your local timezone
- Flexible start and end times outside core hours
- Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week

Leave Policy:
- 20 days paid annual leave per year
- 10 days sick leave per year
- Public holidays are additional to annual leave
- Leave requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance

Code of Conduct:
- Treat all colleagues with respect
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Report any concerns to HR immediately

Equipment Policy:
- Laptop provided on first day
- Additional equipment can be requested through IT
- All company equipment must be returned on last day

Benefits:
- Health insurance covered 100% by company
- Gym membership reimbursement up to $50/month
- Learning budget of $1,000 per year per employee

This document is accessible to all employees.
